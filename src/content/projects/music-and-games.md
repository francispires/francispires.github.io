## Music

Music has been a constant throughout my life. I compose, produce, and experiment with synthesis — mostly instrumental, often influenced by math (polyrhythms, microtonality, generative sequences).

The intersection of music and code is endlessly interesting: MIDI manipulation in Python, generative composition, audio analysis with spectral tools.

## Game Development

I build small experimental games — primarily as a vehicle for exploring procedural generation, cellular automata, and emergent behavior. Nothing shipped commercially, but plenty of interesting systems.

The challenge of games is that they expose complexity quickly: a simple rule set creates unexpected emergent behavior. That's both the frustration and the appeal.
