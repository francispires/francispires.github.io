## Overview

Woodworking is where I step away from the screen and work with my hands. The discipline is surprisingly similar to software engineering: plan carefully, measure twice, understand your material's constraints, and iterate.

## Projects

- Custom furniture pieces designed in SketchUp before cutting
- MDF shelving systems with precision joinery
- Decorative objects from reclaimed wood
- CNC-routed designs (learning process)

## Why It Matters

Building physical things grounds you. When the code is abstract and the problems are invisible, sawdust and wood glue remind you that making things is the point — whether in pixels or pine.
